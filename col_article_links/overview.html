Relevant Links

Below are links relevant to studying past and current trends in science and technology.

CNET is an American media website that publishes reviews, news, articles, blogs, podcasts and videos on technology and consumer electronics globally. CNET also provides users with a catalog of more than 400,000 popular software titles for users to download (though it should be warned to only use this site for exploring software instead of downloading, as CNET Download is fraught with bloatware and spyware).



Digitaltrends.com, "upgrade your lifestyle" slogan of this website says all about this website. It features breaking news for mobile, computer, and gaming devices; home theater systems, and other miscellaneous products such as cars, music, and photography.



Engadget is a multilingual technology blog network with daily coverage of gadgets and consumer electronics. It also posts rumors about the technological world, frequently offers opinions within its stories, and produces the weekly Engadget Podcast that covers tech and gadget news stories that happened during the week.



Gizmodo is a very useful website for gadget lovers. Gizmodo provides the best guides for gadgets, including news for various operating systems such as Apple, Android and Windows.



Reddit is a news aggregate site that provides news by having users bring them to you. Popular topics and articles are filtered towards the top of the newsfeed based on user input (upvotes). Specific articles on future sciences and technology can be found here, although be warned that many articles depict areas that may see effort, but are usually not guaranteed to come to fruition.



Techcrunch is a website that provides information related to new technology and new gadgets. Techcrunch is the leading technology media in the world. The website reviews new internet portals, websites and products, as well as providing the latest technology news.



TheNextWeb.com is a blog that provides technology related content to you. It covers all the topics that are related to new technology, as well as its business and its culture. This site also provides information and news for next generation gadgets.



Wired gives in-depth coverage of future trends in technology. This site provides the latest news and videos on new gadgets, science, entertainment, business, security devices and other technologies. Its venture, webmonkey.com, provides useful tools for web developers and designers.



ZDNet is a business technology news website published by CBS Interactive, TechRepublic and SmartPlanet. This site provides news articles and product reviews with a focus on enterprise information technologies, hardware and software, mobile and security devices, and current research ventures.
