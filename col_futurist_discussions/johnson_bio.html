Brian Johnson Short Bio

Brian David Johnson's keynotes take his career as a futurist into account. Johnson works for Intel Corporation, actively pursuing computing visions for the year 2020. He utilizes ethnographic studies, technological and trend data, and occasionally sci-fi, in order to bolster Intel's future vision for consumers and their computing needs. Johnson has been instrumental in improving and evolving fields such as artificial intelligence and robotics.

Johnson's written contributions, which include sci-fi-themed short stories and novels, include "Science Fiction Prototyping: Designing with the Future in mind", "Screen Future: The Future of Entertainment Computing and the Devices we Love" and "Fake Plastic Love." Johnson's writing and speaking engagements are all concerned with future trends. Johnson has also forayed into the world of film, directing two feature films.
